Avocado Toast

  • 2 slices bread
  • 1 avocado, mashed
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)

Directions:

Toast the bread until golden brown. Spread the mashed avocado over the toast. Sprinkle with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes (if desired).

Caprese Salad

  • 1 large tomato, sliced
  • 4 oz fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • 1 tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

Arrange the tomato and mozzarella slices on a plate. Drizzle with balsamic vinegar and olive oil. Sprinkle with salt and pepper. Garnish with fresh basil leaves.

Greek Yogurt Parfait

  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup granola
  • 1/2 cup mixed berries
  • 1 tbsp honey

Directions:

Layer the Greek yogurt, granola, and mixed berries in a bowl or jar. Drizzle with honey and serve.

Hummus and Veggie Plate

  • 1 cup hummus
  • Assorted veggies (such as sliced cucumbers, carrots, and bell peppers)

Directions:

Arrange the hummus and veggies on a plate. Serve immediately.

Tuna Salad

  • 1 can tuna, drained
  • 1/4 cup diced celery
  • 1/4 cup diced red onion
  • 2 tbsp mayo
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions:

In a bowl, mix together the tuna, celery, red onion, mayo, salt, and pepper. Serve on top of salad greens or as a sandwich filling.
